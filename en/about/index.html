This is a web-app developed to facilitate searching for the lyrics of your favorite songs, Alina.app fulfills two fundamental purposes:

For the user

You can find all lyrics of your favorite songs in a rapidly, simple and pleasant way. At the same time this software gives you aditional information about the artist you are looking for. All in the same app!

If you like this project, you are encouraged to share it with everyone you know and you think it would help them, this project will keep for free as long as the posibilities allow it

Also we encourage you to send us a comment or feedback about this app, your opinion is very important for the developer

In the near future there will be updates with better features
